About us

Dobi Steel Fixing is an experienced steel reinforcement contractor offering comprehensive steel fixing services to our clients across various sectors of the construction industry. We operate throughout the eastern states and have contributed to some of Australia’s most iconic projects. Our expertise in steel reinforcement ensures that we consistently deliver high-quality results to our contractors, always on time and within budget. We are committed to full compliance with Quality and Safety standards. At Dobi Steel Fixing, we prioritize client service, safety, and quality. We strive to exceed expectations by delivering every project on time, within budget, and with the highest standards of excellence.
